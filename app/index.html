Aims

We aim to provide opportunities for a diverse and inclusive group of Goldsmiths Hackers (henceforth known as 'Hacksmiths') to interact, collaborate, share knowledge, and create things with one another, and a creative environment conducive to such activities.

Objectives

We'll host workshops and hack days for Goldsmiths students and the wider UK student hacker community. We will also organise trips to take part in hack days managed by other hacking societies and organisations, as well as other fun visits.
